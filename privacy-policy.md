# LegacyVault AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 4, 2026

LegacyVault AI helps users organize estate-planning notes, asset records, beneficiary details, executor information, digital asset reminders, document locations, voice legacy notes, and PDF review summaries. LegacyVault AI is an educational organization tool and is not a law firm or legal adviser.

## Data Collection

LegacyVault AI does not collect personal data from users for developer-operated analytics, advertising, tracking, sale, or profiling.

The information users enter in the app is stored locally on the user's device using Apple's local app storage technologies. This may include estate profile details, asset records, beneficiary and executor notes, digital asset notes, document-location notes, family legacy messages, voice recording metadata, transcripts, review summaries, and generated PDF reports.

The developer does not receive, store, sell, or share this local vault content.

## Purchases

LegacyVault AI offers subscriptions through Apple in-app purchases. Purchases, renewals, refunds, and related subscription events are processed by Apple. The developer does not receive full payment card details.

## Microphone, Speech, and Local Files

If a user chooses to use voice legacy features, the app may request microphone and speech recognition permissions. Voice recordings are created only when the user starts recording. Speech recognition may be processed by Apple services according to Apple's own privacy practices. LegacyVault AI does not receive those recordings or transcripts from Apple.

Generated PDF reports are created on device and can be shared by the user through Apple's share sheet.

## Notifications and Biometrics

If enabled, local review reminders are scheduled through Apple notification services. Face ID or Touch ID checks are handled by Apple's LocalAuthentication framework. LegacyVault AI does not receive biometric data.

## AI Features

The current app build uses local placeholder AI-style guidance for estate readiness summaries and voice notes. If future versions connect to a remote AI service in a way that sends user content to the developer or a service provider, this policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated before that collection occurs.

## Data Deletion

Users can delete local app records from the Settings area. Users can also remove app data by deleting the app from their device.

## Children's Privacy

LegacyVault AI is intended for adults organizing family and estate-planning information. It is not directed to children.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as LegacyVault AI evolves. Material changes will be reflected in this document and, where required, in App Store Connect.

## Contact

For privacy questions about LegacyVault AI, contact the developer through the App Store listing or the GitHub repository where this policy is published.
